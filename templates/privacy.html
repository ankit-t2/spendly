Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided during registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history and summaries back to you.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.
  • To send essential service communications, such as password resets.

We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the Service; these providers process data only on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also withdraw consent to data processing at any time, which will result in account termination. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out via the contact information on our site. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
